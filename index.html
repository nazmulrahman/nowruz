What is our GOAL ?

Let's draw some beautiful smiles on this planet


"অবশেষে সব কাজ সেরে আমার দেহের রক্তে নতুন শিশুকে করে যাবো আশীর্বাদ তারপর হবো ইতিহাস"

At present, the countries of the world are very much involved in internal and internal conflicts. In this situation, the basic rights and mental development of children and adolescents, especially poor children and adolescents are under great threat. We can get a new world if we can provide a beautiful life for these children and teenagers. Where every human being is much more aware of himself and his surroundings. With this dream, we are going to start "NOWRUZ" in the hope of a new day.

The purpose of "NOWRUZ" is to help any oppressed people with secularism.

The main target of "NOWRUZ" is poor children and adolescents. Our purpose is not to “donate”; Rather show the way to self-reliance.
